Privacy Policy – Dog Registration App

This page explains how we collect, use, and protect user information when using the Dog Registration mobile application.

Information We Collect

This application collects basic information such as:

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is collected through Facebook Login and is used only for authentication and account management purposes.

How We Use the Information

The collected information is used only to:

  • Allow users to create and access their accounts
  • Provide the functionality of the application
  • Improve the user experience

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data with third parties.

Data Protection

We take reasonable measures to protect user information and ensure that personal data is handled securely.

Account Deletion

Users can delete their account and all associated data directly from the application.

Steps to delete your account

  1. Log in to the application.
  2. Open the menu located in the upper left corner (three horizontal lines).
  3. Select the option "Delete my profile".
  4. Your account and associated data will be permanently deleted.

Once the account is deleted, all personal data associated with the account will be removed from our servers.
